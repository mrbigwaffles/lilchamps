Frequently Asked Questions

Each child develops at their own rate. We will be using the EYFS to track your child's development. If they are behind on their development, we will plan individual activities with your child to help them develop in a certain area, for example: maths.

Yes, we offer flexible session arrangements to suit your family's needs. Please discuss your requirements with us when you visit.

Our private fees vary depending on the age of your child and the number of sessions required. Please contact us for our latest fee schedule.

We encourage a smooth transition period where parents can stay with their child initially. The duration of this will be agreed upon with the key worker based on your child's needs.

Children need to bring their own packed lunches that contain healthy food. We will provide healthy snacks and milk during snack times.

Yes, we organize educational trips and outings throughout the year. Parents will be informed in advance and permission slips will need to be completed.

We require one half-term's notice in writing if you wish to withdraw your child from the nursery.

We have a complaints policy in place. Initially, we encourage you to speak with your child's key worker or the manager. If the matter remains unresolved, please put your complaint in writing to the nursery manager.
